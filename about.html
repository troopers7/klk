About us:

We are excited to share our story with you. Our blood donation organization was established on March 23, 2020, in the heart of Keelakarai. Since then, our dedicated team has worked tirelessly to make a meaningful impact in the lives of those in need. Every blood donation is a chance to bring hope and healing to patients facing critical health challenges. We believe in the power of community and invite you to join us in our mission to save lives.
